About Us

In addition to engineering and design competency and services, also Sharavathi Technologies has specialized in installation and commissioning systems for industries. Sharavathi Technologies has experienced engineers for system design, PLC, SCADA software development, installation, Commissioning & supervision, start-up assistance and operator training. We are particularly experienced designing and building complete plc programming for machines, material handling and related controls and software development. Our experienced team of engineers and supplier partners insure that the solution is on-time and always performs beyond the customer's expectations. Sharavathi Technologies is a nationally recognized company serving several market segments in the India and abroad with key disciplines in custom design equipment, continuous and after sale support and ongoing problem solving skills.
